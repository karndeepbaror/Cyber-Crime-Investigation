
# OSINT Tools Used

This document lists `Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT)` tools commonly
used during cyber crime investigations and digital profiling.

***Social Media & Identity Analysis:***

- **Google Dorking**
  Used to identify public traces of usernames, emails, and leaked data.
- **Instagram / Facebook Manual Analysis**
  Profile metadata, bio patterns, username reuse.
- **Username Enumeration Techniques**
  Cross-platform username correlation (manual & tool-assisted).

***Domain & Infrastructure Intelligence:***

- **WHOIS Lookup**
  Domain registration details `(redacted in reports).|
- **DNS Records Review**
  A, MX, TXT record analysis for phishing detection.
- **Certificate Transparency Logs**
  Detection of suspicious or recently issued SSL certificates.

***Image & Media Analysis:***

- **Reverse Image Search**
  Identifying reused profile pictures or stock images.
- **EXIF Awareness**
  Understanding metadata risks `(no extraction without consent).`

***OSINT Principles Followed:***

- Passive data collection only
- No account interaction
- No bypassing of privacy controls
- Legal & ethical boundaries maintained
