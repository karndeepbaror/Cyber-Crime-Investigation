# Reporting Standards

All case reports in this repository follow `professional documentation standards.`

**Report Structure:**

- Case overview
- Scope and limitations
- Findings
- Evidence summary
- Timeline
- Conclusion

**Writing Principles:**

- Clear and objective language
- No assumptions or speculation
- Fact-based observations only
- Legal and ethical clarity

**Purpose of Reporting:**

- Demonstrate investigation clarity
- Ensure reproducibility
- Maintain professional accountability

Reports are written in a manner suitable for review by:
- `Cyber crime investigators`
- `SOC analysts`
- `Legal and compliance teams`
