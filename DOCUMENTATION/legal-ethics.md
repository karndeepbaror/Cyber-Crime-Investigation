# Legal & Ethical Considerations

All work documented in this repository follows strict `legal and ethical boundaries.`

**Legal Compliance:**

- No unauthorized system access is performed
- No private or confidential data is collected
- No interaction with real victims or suspects
- No circumvention of platform security controls

**Ethical Guidelines:**

- Passive investigation only
- Publicly available information (OSINT)
- No impersonation or social engineering
- No evidence fabrication
