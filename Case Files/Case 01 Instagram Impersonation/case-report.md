# Case Report  
***Case 01: Instagram Impersonation***

**Case Category:**
Social Media Impersonation & Online Identity Misuse

**Investigation Type:**
Simulated Cyber Crime Investigation `(Educational & Ethical)`

**Date of Documentation:**
December 2025

**Investigator:**
Karndeep Baror  
`Digital Forensics & Cyber Crime Investigation` (Portfolio Case)


**1. Case Background**:

An instance of social media impersonation was identified involving a fake
Instagram account created to mimic a legitimate individual. The impersonating
account used a similar username, copied profile images, and misleading profile
information.

The activity had potential to cause `reputational harm` and `social confusion.`


**2. Nature of Complaint (Simulated):**

The affected individual observed:
- Unauthorized use of `personal photographs`
- `Similar username` intended to mislead viewers
- Messages being sent to `known contacts`
- `False representation` of identity


**3. Scope of Investigation:**

- Passive analysis only
- No account access attempts
- No interaction with suspect account
- Only publicly visible information reviewed


**4. Investigation Actions Taken:**

- Profile comparison `(original vs suspected account)`
- Username similarity analysis
- Profile image reuse identification
- Account activity and behavior review



**5. Findings:**

- The impersonating account showed recent creation indicators
- Low follower count and limited activity
- Profile bio text partially copied
- No evidence of direct financial fraud observed


**6. Conclusion:**

The case represents a common social media impersonation pattern.
Early identification and reporting can prevent further misuse.

Status: `CLOSED `
