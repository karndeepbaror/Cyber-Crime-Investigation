# Case Report  
**Case 03: Email Phishing Simulation**

**Case Category:**
Corporate / Credential Harvesting Simulation

**Investigation Type:**
Simulated Cyber Crime Investigation (Educational & Ethical)

**Date of Documentation:**
December 2025

**Investigator:**
Karndeep Baror  
`Digital Forensics & Cyber Crime Investigation` (Portfolio Case)


 **1. Case Background:**

A simulated phishing email was sent to a test corporate account for portfolio purposes.  
Objective: Understand phishing patterns and document investigative response.

No real credentials were accessed; no live company was impacted.



**2. Scope of Investigation:**

- Analyze sender domain and email headers  
- Identify phishing indicators  
- Timeline reconstruction of email delivery and response  
- Ethical, legal simulation only



**3. Investigation Actions Taken:**

- Header analysis: SPF, DKIM, DMARC checks  
- Suspicious link evaluation `(safe sandbox)`
- Red-flag patterns identification  
- Timeline of events



**4. Findings:**

- Sender domain spoofed to appear corporate  
- Phishing language targeted credential submission  
- No compromise occurred due to simulation  
- Indicators follow common phishing templates


**5. Conclusion:**

Simulation demonstrates typical phishing workflow.  
Reporting and documentation highlight investigation methodology.

Status:  `CLOSED`
