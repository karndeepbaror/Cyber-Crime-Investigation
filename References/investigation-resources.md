# Cyber Investigation & Digital Forensics Resources

This document lists trusted learning, reference, and operational resources
used in cybercrime investigation and digital forensic analysis.



**1. Government & Official Sources:**

- Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C)
- CERT-In (Indian Computer Emergency Response Team)
- National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
- Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY)
- Bureau of Police Research & Development (BPR&D)

 

**2. Digital Forensics Standards & Guidelines:**

- ISO/IEC 27037 – Digital Evidence Handling
- ISO/IEC 27041 – Incident Investigation
- ISO/IEC 27042 – Analysis & Interpretation
- ISO/IEC 27043 – Incident Investigation Principles
- NIST SP 800-86 – Integrating Forensics into Incident Response

 

**3. Investigation Methodologies:**

- Chain of Custody Model
- Locard’s Exchange Principle (Digital Context)
- OODA Loop (Observe–Orient–Decide–Act)
- MITRE ATT&CK (Threat Behavior Mapping)

 

**4. Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT):**

- WHOIS & DNS analysis
- Social media footprint analysis
- Metadata examination
- Username & email correlation techniques



**5. Common Forensic Domains:**

- Social Media Investigations
- Email & Phishing Analysis
- Mobile Device Forensics
- Network Log Analysis
- Financial & Crypto Fraud Tracing



**6. Ethical & Legal Boundaries:**

- No unauthorized access
- No account takeover
- No data manipulation
- Passive & lawful investigation only
  
  

**Note:**

All investigations documented in this repository follow `ethical`,
`legal`, and `educational guidelines only.`
